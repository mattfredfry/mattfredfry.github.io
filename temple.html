Temple Emanu-El

Restructure and redesign for the 4th largest Jewish congregation in the country.

Contributed:
Content Strategy
Information Architecture
Project Management

More than 130 years old, this organization has thousands of active members and dozens of nearly autonomous departments, each of which maintains its own corner of the massive website.

We were able to successfully prioritize and reorganize the content into a logical hierarchy and implement a highly flexible CMS that allows a variety of content types without users needing to worry about formatting.

Design by Justin Childress in the service of Switch Creative.
